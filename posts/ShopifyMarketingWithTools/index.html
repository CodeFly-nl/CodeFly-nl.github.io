Market Your Shopify Store by Providing Free Digital Tools

Great content can provide your store reputation and leads. As the popularity of content marketing grows, companies compete on the front of content creation. Engaging content that provides value for the audience can do wonder for your Shopify Store.

But great content by itself is of no value if the attention of your audience is not at your content. Train of Attention, especially online, changes fast.




Potentially powerful way to get leads and build trust with your audience is to use the ideas of content marketing and applying them to relevant tools that your audience may need on a regular basis.

This allows your customers to associate your brand with solutions to their problems and can be a major sourse of your inbound marketing.

Shopify itself uses this way of doing things with good results. They provide a full collection of free tools to drive sales of their platform.



UberSuggest uses this technique to drive consulting clients. NameCheap uses it to sell domain and hosting by providing free logo maker. This is underused but effective way to do Inbound Marketing.
