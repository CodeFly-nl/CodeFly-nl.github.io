Implementing Augmented Reality on Your Shopify Store

Identifying sales objections helps face to face sellers increase their conversion. For online stores, doing the same thing can have even more impact on their sales just due to the potential of scaling these objections can be addressed online.





A survey showed that 62% of customers prefer to physically interact with an item they consider purchasing and prefer offline stores over ecommerce.

49% of respondents like physical stores more than online ones because they adore getting things they pay for right away. This shows the biggest sales objection that online shoppers have in comparison to a physical store.

Augmented Reality solves this sales objection for shoppers on your shopify store by actually allowing them to interact with products.

3 out of 4 People Aged 16-44 Are Aware of AR and many have used it as forms of entertainment.



How to add AR capabilities to your shopify store?

  1. Make sure your theme supports 3D objects.

Brooklyn, Debut and Narrative themes do which are free.

  1. Make sure that your 3D files meet the shopify requirements.
  • Recommended size: Less than 6MB
    • Maximum size: 15MB.
    • File type: GLB
  1. Upload the 3D file on your product page just like adding an image.

Just as you add any image to your product page, you can upload these 3D files and your customers can start using them right away.
